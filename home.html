Reducing Food Waste, One Plate at a Time

Join us in our mission to reduce food waste by connecting people with fresh leftover food from restaurants and cafes.

Our Mission

At Plateful, our mission is to reduce food waste, promote sustainable consumption, and foster community connections. We aim to achieve this by connecting individuals with excess homegrown produce or leftover meals from restaurants and cafes with those who can utilize them.

How It Works

Order Food

Browse and order fresh leftover food from nearby restaurants and cafes.

Scan UPI QR Code

Scan the UPI QR code to make the payment and confirm your order.

Pick Up Food

Visit the restaurant to pick up your fresh leftover food.
